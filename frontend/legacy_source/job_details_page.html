About the Role

As a Network Engineer at EXOSISTECH, you will be part of a high-performance infrastructure team dedicated to maintaining and optimizing our mission-critical networks. You will play a key role in designing, implementing, and securing high-speed data communications systems that support our global tech stack. We are looking for someone with a passion for connectivity and a deep understanding of complex routing and switching architectures.

Key Responsibilities

  • check_circle Maintain and configure Cisco routers and switches for high-availability environments.
  • check_circle Monitor network performance and troubleshoot connectivity issues using advanced diagnostic tools.
  • check_circle Implement security protocols, firewalls, and VPNs to ensure data integrity and protection.
  • check_circle Collaborate with cloud engineers to optimize hybrid network connectivity (AWS/Azure).
  • check_circle Participate in on-call rotation to provide support for critical network outages.
